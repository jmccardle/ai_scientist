### Inclusion Criteria
1. Studies examining AI/LLM awareness capabilities
2. Empirical evaluations of metacognition, self-awareness, social awareness, or situational awareness in AI

4. Benchmark development for awareness evaluation
5. Risk assessments related to AI awareness

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Studies on biological consciousness without AI relevance
2. Pure philosophy papers without empirical AI components
3. Non-English publications
4. Duplicate publications (keep most recent/complete version)
5. Opinion pieces without empirical evidence
6. Studies on narrow AI systems without language capabilities
