# Inclusion Criteria Checklist
## Systematic Review on Anthropomorphism in Dialogue Systems

### Quick Screening Checklist

For each paper, evaluate:

#### 1. RELEVANCE TO DIALOGUE SYSTEMS
- [ ] Involves dialogue systems, conversational AI, chatbots, or voice assistants
- [ ] NOT exclusively about physical robots without dialogue
- [ ] NOT purely technical implementation without user interaction

#### 2. ANTHROPOMORPHISM FOCUS
- [ ] Discusses anthropomorphic features, characteristics, or perceptions
- [ ] Addresses human-likeness in AI systems
- [ ] Examines user reactions to anthropomorphic elements

#### 3. RESEARCH CONTRIBUTION
- [ ] Provides empirical data, theoretical framework, or design guidelines
- [ ] NOT purely opinion or speculation
- [ ] Contains substantive analysis or findings

#### 4. PUBLICATION QUALITY
- [ ] Peer-reviewed or from reputable preprint server
- [ ] Full text available
- [ ] English language
- [ ] Published between 2000-2025

### Specific Topic Indicators

**Include if paper addresses ANY of these:**

1. **Anthropomorphism Theory/Models**
   - ELIZA effect
   - Media Equation theory
   - Computers as Social Actors (CASA)
   - Three-factor theory of anthropomorphism

2. **Design Elements**
   - Voice characteristics
   - Linguistic features
   - Personality/personas
   - Visual representations
   - Gender presentation

3. **User Responses**
   - Trust and credibility
   - Emotional attachment
   - Over-reliance
   - Disclosure behaviors
   - Engagement patterns

4. **Ethical Concerns**
   - Deception and transparency
   - Vulnerable populations
   - Bias and stereotypes
   - Responsibility attribution

5. **Applications/Domains**
   - Customer service
   - Healthcare
   - Education
   - Entertainment
   - Personal assistants

### Screening Decision Categories

**INCLUDE** - Clearly meets criteria
**EXCLUDE** - Clearly fails criteria
**UNSURE** - Borderline case, needs full-text review
**REVIEW** - Potentially important, needs discussion

### Exclusion Reason Codes

- **E1**: Wrong technology (not dialogue systems)
- **E2**: No anthropomorphism focus
- **E3**: Not research (opinion only)
- **E4**: Language not English
- **E5**: Full text unavailable
- **E6**: Duplicate publication
- **E7**: Out of scope (too tangential)
