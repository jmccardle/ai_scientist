# Systematic Literature Review Search Strategy
## Integrated Information Theory (IIT) 4.0 and Related Topics

**Date:** November 14, 2024
**Protocol Version:** 1.0
**PRISMA 2020 Compliant**

---

## Research Question
Conduct a comprehensive systematic literature review of all topics, theoretical frameworks, empirical evidence, and references discussed in or related to the IIT 4.0 paper by Albantakis et al. (2023).

---

## Search Strategy Overview

### Databases to Search
1. **OpenAlex** - Comprehensive multidisciplinary coverage (primary)
2. **arXiv** - Physics, neuroscience, computer science preprints
3. **PubMed** - Biomedical and neuroscience literature
4. **Semantic Scholar** - AI-powered citation network analysis
5. **PhilPapers** - Philosophy of mind and consciousness (if accessible)

### Date Range
- **1990 to November 2024** (to capture full IIT development history)

## Inclusion Criteria

### Required:
1. **Language:** English (primary), will note key non-English works if seminal
2. **Date:** 1990 - November 2024
3. **Relevance:** Must discuss at least ONE of:
   - Integrated Information Theory (any version)
   - Consciousness theories/frameworks
   - Phenomenology of consciousness
   - Mathematical models of consciousness
   - Empirical studies of consciousness
   - Philosophical foundations of consciousness
   - Authors from IIT collaboration

### Publication Types Included:
- Peer-reviewed journal articles
- Conference proceedings
- Book chapters
- Dissertations/theses
- Preprints (arXiv, bioRxiv, PsyArXiv)
- Technical reports
- Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
- Theoretical papers and commentaries
- Empirical studies (all designs)

---

## Exclusion Criteria

1. **Non-English publications** (unless seminal/frequently cited)
2. **Popular media articles** (magazines, newspapers)
3. **Blog posts and informal web content**
4. **Conference abstracts** without full papers
5. **Editorials** without substantive theoretical content
6. **Pure information theory** without consciousness connection
7. **Pure neuroscience** without theoretical consciousness framework
8. **Duplicate publications** (keep most complete version)

---

## Special Search Procedures

### 1. Snowball/Citation Chaining
- Forward citation search from key IIT papers
- Backward citation search (references) from IIT 4.0 paper
- Co-citation analysis for related theoretical frameworks

### 2. Grey Literature
- PhD dissertations on IIT (ProQuest if accessible)
- Technical reports from consciousness research centers
- Preprints not yet peer-reviewed

### 3. Author Tracking
- All papers by key IIT researchers
- Collaborative networks around Tononi lab

### 4. Controversy/Critique Tracking
- Papers explicitly critiquing IIT
- Response papers to critiques
- Comparative analyses of consciousness theories

---

## Quality Control

### Search Validation:
1. **Test searches** with known papers to verify retrieval
2. **Search string refinement** based on initial results
3. **Documentation** of exact search strings and dates
4. **Deduplication** across databases

### Screening Reliability:
1. **Pilot screening** of first 20 papers to refine criteria
2. **Inter-rater reliability** simulation (κ > 0.6 required)
3. **Conflict resolution** protocol for borderline cases
