### Ethics Statement
> ResearchPilot is designed to accelerate legitimate academic research while maintaining scientific rigor. It enforces RULE 1 (no aspirational claims without evidence), follows PRISMA 2020 guidelines, and emphasizes reproducibility. > - Make your theoretical insights
